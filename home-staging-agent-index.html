Consistency plan
🌱
Starter: 3× per week (Mon, Wed, Fri)
The minimum to keep the algorithm warm. Sustainable indefinitely — great while building your filming routine.
📈
Growth: 5× per week (add Tue + Thu)
Doubles your discovery surface. Batch-film 5 videos on Sunday — one session covers the whole week.
🚀
Breakout: daily for 30 days
Algorithms prioritise daily posters in search and discovery. This is when channels break out — then drop to 5×/week to maintain.
Reach tactics for home staging
🏷️
Use real estate + decor hashtag stacks
Mix #homestaging #interiorstyling #realestate with suburb tags like #melbournehomes. Sellers find staging content through property searches.
↔️
Before/after is your highest-converting format
Film your own rooms styled vs unstyled. Viewers share these and they directly show your staging value to potential clients.
🤝
Tag local real estate agents
Tag agents in decor tip videos. When they share to their audience, their seller clients become warm staging leads for you.
📩
Add a booking CTA in every video
Point to a simple "Book a staging consult" link in bio. Start collecting interested leads now — even before you officially launch.

Filming your home — iPhone tips
☀️
Natural light at golden hour (7–9am / 4–6pm)
Face your window while filming. Cloudy days give soft, flattering light — perfect for talking-head or close-up detail shots.
📐
Pick 2–3 signature spots in your home
Viewers build familiarity with your backgrounds. You save setup time — walk in, film, done. Your home becomes your brand.
📱
iPhone on a tripod is all you need
Film vertical at 4K. Use the back camera (better quality) and a small mirror to check framing. A cheap ring light handles evening shots.
